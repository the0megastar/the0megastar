# Hello, I'm Mike 👋

I'm a software developer based in the Lehigh Valley, PA. I spent nearly a decade 
at Apple across mentoring, technical support, site support engineering, and UAT coordination 
before finishing my CS degree and going all-in on building my own software.

I lean toward privacy-first, local apps that work for the user, usually because I'm solving a problem I have myself. I might be a little sentimental, but I miss the days of buying software once and having it live on your computer with your data where it belongs. That model doesn't make sense for every business, but it's my focus. I also believe software should be platform agnostic, and that's the direction I'm heading. I have a background in graphic design too, which means I tend to think about how something looks and feels before I write a line of code.
